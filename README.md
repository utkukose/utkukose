### 🎓 About Me

I am a **Full Professor of Computer Engineering** and AI researcher working at the intersection of **Explainable AI (XAI)**, **Digital Twins**, **interdisciplinary AI applications**, **AI in biomedical / healthcare** and **decision support systems**.

- 🏫 **Süleyman Demirel University** — Isparta, Turkey *(primary affiliation)*
- 🌎 **Universidad Panamericana** — Mexico City, Mexico
- 🇺🇸 **University of North Dakota** — Grand Forks, USA
- 🇮🇳 **VelTech University** — Chennai, India

📌 Currently leading **STING DSS** (TÜBİTAK 1001 No. 123E383) — a clinical decision support system for pediatric acute lymphoblastic leukemia drug repositioning using **Digital Twins** and **Deep Learning**.

🏅 **SM'IEEE** (Senior Member) · **M'ACM** (Professional Member) · Associate Editor, *IEEE Access*
